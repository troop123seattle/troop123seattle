Join the Adventure with Seattle's Troop 123

Ready to embark on an exciting journey filled with outdoor adventures, leadership opportunities and lifelong friendships? Look no further than Scouts BSA Troop 123


As an integral part of the renowned Scouts BSA program, Troop 123 is dedicated to:

  • empowering young individuals
  • fostering growth
  • preparing youth for a future of excellence

Turning wild, untamed kids into Eagle Scouts since 1929

Located in Northwest Seattle, Troop 123 is chartered by the Whitman Middle School PTA.




We get outdoors at least once a month (rain, snow or shine).

Nature is Our Teacher

At Scouts BSA Troop 123, we believe in the transformative power of the great outdoors. Our scouts are constantly exploring and embracing nature's wonders.


From paddling on serene Lake Washington to taking invigorating and breathtaking hikes, our troop immerses scouts in many local outdoor activities. Join us for rewarding activities that foster a deep appreciation for the natural world.

Beyond local adventures, Troop 123 ventures to renowned Scouts camps like Parsons, Pigott, Shepard, Meriwether and Easton. Scouts hone their skills, forge lifelong friendships and create lasting memories.

Whether it's camping under starry skies near Lake Wenatchee or exploring historic Fort Ebey, you can embark on unforgettable outdoor experiences that inspire a love for adventure and a spirit of exploration. Join Troop 123 and discover the joy of outdoor adventure today!

Why Choose Troop 123

  • Experienced Leadership: Our dedicated team of adult leaders brings years of scouting experience and a passion for guiding young individuals.

  • Strong Community: Troop 123 is more than just a group of scouts. Join a supportive community where scouts forge friendships and make memories.

  • Focus on Growth: Through a comprehensive program, you can develop essential life skills, build confidence and unlock your full potential.

  • Tradition of Excellence: With a rich history of scouting excellence since 1947, Troop 123 upholds the values of the Scouts BSA program and strives for excellence in all endeavors.
